Every Auriga account comes with a virtual Mastercard, ready the moment you're approved.

## Use your virtual card

Add it to Apple Pay or Google Pay, or use the card details for online purchases. It works anywhere Mastercard is accepted.

## Order a physical card

Go to **Card → Order physical card**. Delivery typically takes 5–7 business days.

## Freeze and unfreeze

If you misplace your card, open **Card** and tap **Freeze**. This instantly blocks new payments. Unfreeze just as quickly when you find it. We'll email you whenever your card is frozen or unfrozen so you always know its status.

## Spending abroad

Use your card worldwide. Non-EUR transactions are converted at the Mastercard exchange rate with no hidden Auriga markup.
