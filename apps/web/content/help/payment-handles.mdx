A payment handle is a short, memorable username — like `@michael` — that people can use to pay you instead of copying your IBAN.

## Claim your handle

You can claim a handle during onboarding, or any time from **Settings → Payment handle**. Handles are unique, 3–20 characters, and can contain letters, numbers, and underscores.

## Send money by handle

1. Open **Send**.
2. Enter a recipient's `@handle` and tap **Look up**.
3. Confirm the resolved name, enter an amount, and send.

## Request money by handle

When you create a payment request, your `@handle` is shown on the public payment page so the sender knows exactly who they're paying.

> Tip: a clear, recognizable handle reduces mistakes and builds trust when you ask to be paid.
