Security is built into Auriga from the ground up. Here's how your account stays protected — and how you can help.

## Passwordless sign-in

Auriga uses one-time codes instead of passwords, so there's no password to leak or reuse. Each sign-in is verified with a fresh code sent to your email.

## How your money is held

Your funds are safeguarded in dedicated accounts at regulated European credit institutions, kept separate from Auriga and Swan operational funds, in line with EU e-money regulations.

## Staying safe

- Auriga will never ask for your one-time code over the phone or by email.
- Freeze your card instantly from the **Card** screen if it's lost.
- Review your transaction history regularly and report anything unexpected from **Support**.

If you ever suspect fraud, contact us immediately through the in-app Support page.
