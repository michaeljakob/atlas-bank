Atlas keeps transfers fast and transparent.

## Transfer times

- **SEPA Instant:** under 10 seconds, including weekends and holidays.
- **Standard SEPA:** within one business day.

When an instant transfer isn't supported by the receiving bank, Atlas automatically falls back to a standard SEPA transfer.

## Fees

EUR-to-EUR SEPA transfers are free or near-free, with no hidden exchange-rate markup. When you convert between currencies, you get the real exchange rate with a clear, upfront fee — never a buried spread.

## Limits

Transfer limits depend on your verification level. You can view your current limits in **Settings**.
