Welcome to Auriga. This guide walks you through everything from opening your account to making your first payment.

## Open your account

1. Enter your email — we'll send a one-time code instead of asking for a password.
2. Add your name, date of birth, nationality, and country of residence.
3. Verify your identity with a photo of your ID and a quick selfie.

Most accounts are approved in seconds. Once approved, your EUR IBAN and a virtual Mastercard are ready immediately.

## Fund your account

Send a SEPA transfer to your new IBAN, or ask someone to pay you using your IBAN or `@handle`. Incoming SEPA Instant transfers arrive within seconds.

## Make your first transfer

Go to **Send**, choose a recipient (by IBAN or `@handle`), enter an amount, and confirm. That's it.

Need a hand? Reach our team any time from the in-app **Support** page.
