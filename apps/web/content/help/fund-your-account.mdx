There are a few ways to get money into your Auriga account.

## By SEPA transfer

Send money from any European bank to your Auriga IBAN. You'll find your IBAN on the **Dashboard** and in **Settings**.

- **SEPA Instant** transfers arrive in under 10 seconds, 24/7.
- **Standard SEPA** transfers arrive within one business day.

## By receiving a payment

Share your IBAN — or your `@handle` — with whoever is paying you. With a handle, they can pay you without typing a long account number.

## How long does it take?

Incoming instant transfers are available immediately. Standard transfers settle on the next business day. You'll get a notification (and an email, if enabled) the moment funds arrive.
