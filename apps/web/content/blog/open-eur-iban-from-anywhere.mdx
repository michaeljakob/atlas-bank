Opening a euro account used to mean a branch visit, proof of a local address, and weeks of waiting. Today you can get a real, dedicated EUR IBAN online in about a minute. Here's exactly how it works with Atlas.

## What is a EUR IBAN, really?

An IBAN (International Bank Account Number) is your account's address for sending and receiving money across the SEPA zone. A **dedicated** IBAN is issued in your name — not a pooled sub-account shared with thousands of other users. That distinction matters: salary payments, direct debits, and refunds all expect an account that unambiguously belongs to you.

## Opening your account, step by step

1. **Enter your email.** We send a one-time code — no passwords to forget.
2. **Add your details.** Name, date of birth, nationality, and residence. This takes about a minute.
3. **Verify your identity.** A quick photo of your ID and a selfie. Most checks complete in seconds.
4. **Get your IBAN and card.** Your EUR IBAN is live immediately and a virtual Mastercard is ready to use.

> No local address required. You can open from anywhere in the European Economic Area.

## What you can do next

- Receive your salary or invoices straight to your IBAN.
- Send money over **SEPA Instant** — arriving in under 10 seconds, 24/7.
- Pay anywhere Mastercard is accepted, online and in store.
- Claim a **payment handle** so friends can pay you by `@handle` instead of typing an IBAN.
